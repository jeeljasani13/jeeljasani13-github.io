Introduction

I designed a poster to present a new Controls Design. I redesigned the ID Card to enable easier entry into buildings in Carnegie Mellon University.

Tools: Problem Identification, Brainstorming, Persona and Scenario Building, Storyboarding, Low / Mid / High Fidelity Prototype, Poster Presentation.

The Problem

The ID card swiping system for entering college buildings is not the most ideal. Especially in Pittsburgh winters when you are wearing gloves, it is cold and uncomfortable to remove the gloves, find and swipe the ID card to enter the campus buildings. It is even more inconvenient when you're carrying numerous bags or books and your hands are full and you just cannot reach for your ID card even if it is just in your pocket.
