This game is based on a challenge faced by the players and a cultural preference of the players. Our challenge is that players need permission from their parents to play and our cutural preference is that they exclude noobs from the game. Starting from brainstorms of each of these aspects individually to coming up with our final game, we iterated through numerous ideas and below, I have documented this entire process through notes, descriptions, and photographs.

The Game

Compete and cooperate with others by using simple mathematical operations to force opponents to hit their ‘death number’ or to hit your own ‘target number’. The player who hits their target number first, or is the last player remaining, wins.
