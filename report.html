I designed a formal report for the Interaction Design Fundamentals class. I was a given a text on Industrial Ecology and had to convert it into a meaningful report for my chosen clients. I picked the International Society for Industrial Ecology as my client. My stakeholders were the committee of the society as well as environmental activists.

I designed the layout, information architecture, grid system, typography, images, color, and tone of voice for the report to cater to my chosen clients.

Tools: Grid Systems, Typography, Color, Visual Design, Infographics, Sketching, Content Layout, Information Architecture, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator.
